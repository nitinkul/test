DOUBLES and MIXED DOUBLES CHAMPIONSHIP

  1. All matches upto the quarterfinals will be matches of 3 sets each, with each set consisting of 15 points.
  2. The semis and the finals will be matches of 3 sets each, with 21 points in each set.
  1. The matches will be conducted as per the standard rules of badminton.
  2. Game begins with participant serving from the right side of the court.
  3. Odd point’s service will be done from the left side of the court.
  4. The scoring practice shall be the continuous scoring system, wherein points are won on service as well as by breaking the opponent's service.
  5. The set is declared over when one participant has a clear two point majority over his opponent, in case of a deuce situation, or when one of the participant has reached the Game scores of 11,15 or 21 (depending on which round of the tournament.)
  6. A score of 7­0, in 11 point and 15 point sets, or a score of 11­ 0, in a 21 point set, for the a participant shall be declared a win.
  7. In case of disturbance, like wind, the referee shall take a decision whether to 'let' the point or re­conduct the point.
  8. The referee and the linesmen's calls will be considered in full and as final in awarding points.
